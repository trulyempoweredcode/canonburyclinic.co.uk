Stretching & Exercise

What happens between sessions matters just as much as what happens during them.

Assisted Stretching

Targeted Stretching That Starts With the Right Diagnosis

Stretching sounds simple, but doing it properly requires understanding exactly what is going on in your body first. A correct diagnosis is essential — in some cases, stretching can actually be contra-indicative, making the problem worse rather than better.

Your osteopath begins by understanding how each muscle is functioning, then isolates it precisely to deliver an accurate, effective stretch. This is not generic flexibility work — it is clinical stretching informed by a thorough assessment of your musculoskeletal system.

During your treatment session, assisted stretching is used to reduce tension, increase joint mobility, and prepare tissues for recovery. You will also be given specific home stretches tailored to your condition, so the progress you make in clinic continues between appointments.

Why It Matters

The Benefits of Proper Stretching

  • Reduces muscle tension and stiffness
  • Increases joint mobility and range of motion
  • Targets specific muscles based on clinical assessment
  • Applied during treatment and prescribed for home use
  • Avoids contra-indicative stretches that could worsen your condition
Exercise Programme

Bespoke Exercises to Get You Out of Pain and Keep You There

Getting you out of pain is only half the job. Keeping you out of pain — restoring muscular balance and preventing repeat injury — requires the right exercises, done consistently at home.

Every patient is different, so every exercise programme is bespoke. Your osteopath will design a combination of strengthening and mobilisation exercises tailored to your body, your condition, and your goals. The aim is simple: rebuild the strength and balance your body needs to function properly and stay injury-free.

You will receive clear instructions for each exercise — through printed handouts or video access — so you can follow your programme with confidence between sessions.

Recovery Does Not Stop at the Clinic Door

The right stretches and exercises turn a good treatment into a lasting result. Get in touch and let’s build a plan that works for your body.
