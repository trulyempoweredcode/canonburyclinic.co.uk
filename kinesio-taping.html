Kinesio Taping

Lightweight support that moves with your body — applied by a certified RockDoc practitioner.

What Is Kinesio Taping?

Precision Support Without Restriction

Kinesiology tape is a fine, breathable, stretchable material made from cotton or a cotton blend. It is applied directly to the skin using a medically approved adhesive that is water-proof, sweat-proof, latex-free, and available in hypoallergenic options. Once applied, it stays in place for three to four days — through showers, exercise, and everyday life.

Unlike rigid strapping, kinesiology tape is designed to maintain support while allowing free movement of blood and lymphatic fluid beneath the skin. It stabilises the injured area without locking it down, allowing connective tissue to move naturally with the body. Many patients report noticeable results within 24 hours of application.

How It Works

What Kinesio Tape Does for Your Body

The tape works on multiple levels simultaneously, making it a versatile tool in your recovery:

  • Improves circulation to the injured area
  • Supports muscles without restricting range of motion
  • Allows free flow of blood and lymphatic fluid beneath the skin
  • Stabilises the injury while the body heals
  • Prevents further injury while still allowing full movement
  • Stays on for 3–4 days through showers, sport and daily activity
Certified Practitioner

Applied by a Qualified RockDoc

Hussein is a certified RockDoc through RockTape Inc., having completed multiple accredited courses in kinesiology taping techniques. This means your tape is applied with precision — the right tension, the right direction, and the right placement for your specific injury and body mechanics.

Kinesio taping is used as part of your broader treatment plan at Canonbury Clinic. It is not a standalone service but an additional support that extends the benefit of your osteopathic treatment between sessions, keeping you moving and healing around the clock.

Support That Works While You Do

Kinesio tape keeps working long after you leave the clinic — supporting your recovery through every shower, workout, and night’s sleep. Get in touch to find out if taping is right for you.
