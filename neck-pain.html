Neck Pain Treatment

Expert diagnosis and hands-on treatment for neck pain, stiffness and trapped nerves in Islington.

We Understand

Neck Pain Affects Everything You Do

If you’re dealing with neck pain, you know it’s more than just a stiff neck. It can stop you turning your head to drive, make it impossible to concentrate at work, and leave you dreading another sleepless night propped up on pillows.

Whether it came on suddenly or has been building for months, neck pain has a way of taking over your daily life. The good news is that it doesn’t have to stay that way.

Understanding Your Pain

What Causes Neck Pain?

There are many different causes of neck pain, and it often doesn’t have one simple explanation. The important thing to remember is that pain doesn’t necessarily mean there’s a serious problem — but it does deserve proper attention.

Some of the specific conditions commonly linked with neck pain include:

  • Muscle spasm — sudden, involuntary tightening of the neck muscles
  • Trapped nerve — compression that can cause pain, numbness, or tingling down the arm
  • Postural problems — strain from prolonged desk work, phone use, or poor sleeping positions
  • Slipped disc — a cervical disc that bulges and irritates surrounding structures
  • Spondylosis — age-related wear and tear of the cervical spine
  • General wear and tear — gradual degeneration that accumulates over time

The sooner you get the correct treatment, the less time you spend in pain. That’s why a quick, accurate diagnosis is always our first priority.
