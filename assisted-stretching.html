Assisted Stretching

Guided stretching that takes you deeper than you can go alone — for real, lasting flexibility.

What Is Assisted Stretching?

Going Beyond What You Can Achieve on Your Own

Assisted stretching is a hands-on technique where your osteopath guides and facilitates the stretching of specific muscles and muscle groups during your treatment session. Unlike static stretching you might do at home, assisted stretching uses gentle, controlled external force to reach deeper layers of muscle tissue.

The effectiveness lies in engaging your body’s proprioceptive mechanisms and neuromuscular pathways. By providing expert guidance, your practitioner can help override your body’s protective stretch reflex — allowing for a deeper, more effective stretch without triggering the reflexive muscle contractions that limit what you can achieve alone.

Why It Works

The Benefits of Assisted Stretching

  • Greater flexibility and range of motion than static stretching alone
  • Targeted stretching of specific muscle groups to address your individual needs
  • Reduced muscle tension and stress for an overall sense of calm
  • Improved movement patterns for sport, fitness, and daily life
  • Lower risk of injury through optimised flexibility and muscle balance
  • Enhanced athletic performance — greater speed, agility, and power output
During Your Session

What to Expect

Assisted stretching is incorporated into your osteopathic treatment session as part of a tailored approach to your specific musculoskeletal issues. Your practitioner will identify which muscles need attention and apply gentle, rhythmic movements to elongate and relax them.

The process is collaborative and largely enjoyable. The gentle, rhythmic movements promote relaxation while delivering real therapeutic results. Your osteopath will tailor each stretch to your individual needs, ensuring proper technique and safety throughout.

Whether you’re an athlete looking to improve performance, recovering from an injury, or simply want to move more freely in everyday life, assisted stretching can make a meaningful difference.

Who Benefits

Assisted Stretching Is Ideal For

  • Athletes wanting to optimise performance
  • People recovering from injuries
  • Office workers with tight muscles from sitting
  • Anyone with restricted mobility or stiffness
  • Older adults wanting to maintain flexibility
  • Fitness enthusiasts looking to improve range of motion
